Peptide Spectrum Matching

This tool selects potential peptides for each measured spectrum and computes theoretical spectra for them. Those theoretical spectra are then compared to the measured specctrum and scored according to their similarity. This way, the most likely peptide from which the measured spectrum originated from can be identified using the similarity scores.
